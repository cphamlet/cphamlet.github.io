SCH MEDDLE

About

Schedule Meddle is a revolutionary web based application that will take any of the complicated syllabi given to you by your professors and automatically upload it to your Google Calendar! Our goal is to help students organize their lives in order to accomplish all tasks with efficiencey, percision, and excellence. Schedule Meddle also helps students who may not have the ability to plan out their every day activities. With a few clicks, Schedule Meddle can make any student's life more organized! The program takes pdf files as input.

Please click one of the Google Calendar links listed below or open Google Calendar itself!
